# Box-art template credits

Generic console case templates that overlay box-art framing onto game covers
(see `main/components/bigpicture/BpmBoxArtOverlay.vue`). Each is a transparent
PNG: the console's case frame is opaque, the cover slot is transparent.

Templates added for this project, sourced from free fan-made cover templates
(personal use, credit to the creators):

- `ps1.png` — "PS1 Cover Template HD Remake" by **brfa98** (DeviantArt)
- `n64.png` — "N64 Cover Art Template AIO" by **koobie777** (DeviantArt); the plain top-right variant cropped out of the all-in-one sheet
- `switch.png` — "Nintendo Switch Box Template with Spine Shade" by **goldmetalsonic** (DeviantArt)

The other templates in this folder predate the above and are unattributed.

These case designs are trademarks of their respective owners (Sony, Nintendo)
and are used here only to frame each console's games. If you fork or distribute
this publicly, replace them with assets you have licensed.
