# Console art credits

The isometric console renders in this folder power the emulation (console)
library view. They are the work of their respective creators:

- **Jude Coram** — https://www.judecoram.com/pixel-art-game-consoles/
  The flat-shaded isometric renders: PlayStation 1 / 2 / 4, SNES, NES, N64,
  Wii, Wii U, Xbox, Xbox 360, Dreamcast, Sega Genesis (Mega Drive), Master
  System, Sega Saturn, Atari 2600, ZX Spectrum. (Backgrounds removed for use.)

- **es-theme-pixel** — the "Pixel" EmulationStation theme by **Rookervik** and
  **ehettervik** (https://github.com/ehettervik/es-theme-pixel), with console
  additions by **TheGemsbok** (https://github.com/RetroPie/es-theme-pixel/pull/10).
  The pixel renders for: GameCube, Game Boy Advance, Game Boy Color, Switch,
  PlayStation 3, Nintendo 3DS. The theme's README grants: "Feel free to modify
  this theme or use graphics in other projects."

- **Official console logos** (`logos/`) — the official brand logos, sourced
  from Wikimedia Commons. Most keep their brand colours on the cards; the
  monochrome ones (PlayStation wordmark, GameCube, Switch, Wii) are filtered to
  white so they read on the dark background. The Switch logo is the official
  "Nintendo Switch logo transparent + wordmark" PNG (icon + wordmark lockup);
  the PlayStation logo is the "PlayStation Wordmark" SVG. These are trademarks
  of their respective owners (Sony, Nintendo, Microsoft, Sega, Atari) and are
  used here only to label each console.

This is a private, non-commercial personal project. If you fork or distribute
it publicly, replace these with assets you have licensed (for example,
commission Jude Coram) or a clean CC0 set.
